Tips for using my website and entering giveaways

  • Press ctrl + w to close a finished giveaway or when visiting a required website.
  • Macro ctrl + w to a mouse button if you can.
  • Fast forward through youtube videos by clicking to the end.
  • Fast forward through youtube videos by pausing then holding down the right arrow key.
  • Open 12-14 links at the same time.
  • If an open giveaway has a time consuming task move to the other side of the opened tabs.
  • Looking for specific keywords? Press crtl + f to open the search.
  • Accidentally close a tab? Press ctrl + shift + t.
